F.A.Q

Flat Rate Shipping

 Moroccanoil orders are shipped via UPS on business days (Monday through Friday 8:30-17:30, excluding major holidays). Moroccanoil offers standard shipping to the contiguous 48 US states. free shipping is offered on all orders excluding Alaska and Hawaii. For Alaska and Hawaii, Moroccanoil offers a $35 flat rate shipping fee for all orders. Shipments to U.S territories (such as Puerto Rico and Guam) and the Armed Forces or to a P.O. Box are currently unavailable. Please allow 5-10 business days for the processing and shipping of an order.

Return Policy

 Moroccanoil does not accept any returns for products purchased on the E-Store. Products purchased on the E-Store cannot be returned to a Moroccanoil distributor nor to a salon which sells Moroccanoil products. If a product purchased is defective, customer shall contact Moroccanoil Online Customer Service by phone at 1-888-800-1888 , or via email at info@moroccanoil.com , within 14 days of receipt of product. In order to process its request, the customer shall provide a detailed description of the defect and a proof of purchase.
