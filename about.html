Assignment:

For this project you need to work with another person to design and develop a website about the history of the Internet. Each person should submit their work for the assignment and each person needs to have made commits to the project's repository. You will need to have at least 5 pages and design the site with either an F layout or Z layout. You will need to select a color scheme, fonts, and graphics to go along with the theme of your website. In addition to designing your website, you need to create a persona that describes a typical user of your website that is representative of the audience that you are targeting. You should submit a link to the website, your Github repository, and a PowerPoint presentation that describes the purpose of the website, your audience, contains screen shots of the website, and your persona.
